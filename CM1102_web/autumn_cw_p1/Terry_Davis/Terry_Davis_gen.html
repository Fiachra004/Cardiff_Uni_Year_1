Early life

Terrence Davis was born in Wisconsin. He was the seventh of eight children, and his father was an industrial enginer. From a young age Terrence showed interest in computers, As a child Davis used an Apple II. Whilst still in his teens he learnt how to write programs in assembly language. Obviously aware of his intelligence, but also being able to show humility as seen in his quote "I'm a rocket scientist, though, not a very good one"

Mental Illness

1996 Davis was sent to a psychiatric ward every 6 months due to manic episodes, he would leave his home due to fear of people following him which would lead to him being reamitted into a psychiatric ward. He would be diagnosed with schizophrenia, this would affect his personal projects where he would go on to make "TempleOS" an Operating system that Davis belives god told him to build. Thoughout the development of templeOS Davis believed that he was under constant persecution from the CIA. He was considered controversial due to his use of both homophobic and racial slurs, which many people put down to his mental Illness. After completion of TempleOS, he would post blogs, were he referred to himself as the "Smartest programmer that's ever lived".

Death

In the last months of his life, Davis would go between being homless and in prison. He stopped taking his medication as he believed that it limited his creativity. He was offered housing by his fans however he would refuse. In his final video, he explained that he has removed his videos as he did not wish to "litter" the internet. On the evening of August 11, 2018, while walking alongside railroad tracks, Davis was strucj and killed by Union Pacific train. Invetigators could not decided whether his death was suicide or accidental, however the train engineer belived his death to be suicide.
